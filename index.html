We Are Southern Run Club

Welcome to Southern Run Club, where running isn't just a sport – it's a way of life. Based in the heart of the South, our run club brings together people of all backgrounds and fitness levels to share in the joy of running, support one another, and embrace the rich traditions and warm hospitality of our community. Whether you're an experienced marathoner or just starting your running journey, you'll find a supportive, welcoming group here. We offer a variety of runs – from casual jogs to more competitive events – all designed to challenge and inspire you to reach your personal best. Our mission is to promote fitness, encourage camaraderie, and connect runners with the beautiful landscapes and trails that the South has to offer. From scenic runs through charming Southern towns to weekend training sessions and social events, there's always something to look forward to at Southern Run Club. Join us, and be part of a passionate community that celebrates running, friendship, and the spirit of the South.

Events

Southern Sunrise Fun Run

Start the day with a beautiful sunrise run through a scenic route, followed by coffee and breakfast at a local café. This casual event is perfect for runners of all levels who want to enjoy the peaceful morning atmosphere.

The Southern Half Marathon

Host an annual half marathon through local streets, parks, or along the coastline. A signature event for more serious runners, this race could also include shorter distances (5k, 10k) to accommodate all participants.

Tasting & Trail Run

A fun, themed event that combines running with local Southern food culture. Participants run through a scenic trail, with pit stops offering small tastings of local dishes or snacks. Finish with a social gathering to enjoy Southern hospitality.

Night Owl 5k

A unique nighttime race where runners gather for a 5k under the stars. Lights, music, and glowing accessories add to the fun, with post-race celebrations featuring music and snacks.

Family Fun Run & Walk

A family-friendly event designed for runners, walkers, and strollers. This event promotes fitness for all ages, encouraging families to come together and stay active while enjoying a relaxed run or walk through a local park or neighborhood.

Seasonal Charity Run

A run organized for a good cause, held during different seasons. Participants can sign up and donate to a local charity, with a festive twist. Whether it’s a Thanksgiving Turkey Trot, a Christmas Charity Run, or a springtime event supporting a local cause, it’s a great way to give back to the community.

Southern Trail Challenge

A challenging off-road event that takes runners through some of the South’s most scenic and rugged terrain. This event can be either a timed race or a casual group run, encouraging runners to explore the natural beauty of their region while pushing their limits.
